Frequently Asked

Does it work without internet?
Yes. After the first setup download, EduMind works completely offline. No WiFi, no data needed — ever again.
Which Windows versions does it support?
Windows 10 and Windows 11, both 64-bit. The PC needs at least 4GB RAM and 10GB free disk space.
Is it aligned to the new Uganda curriculum?
Yes. EduMind is built around the NCDC 2025 competency-based curriculum for both O-Level and A-Level, including all 29 A-Level subjects.
Can multiple students use it at the same time?
With the School License, up to 5 PCs can be installed. Each PC handles one student session at a time.
How do we get it installed?
Download the file, double click it, follow the steps — exactly like installing any normal software. For School License customers, we come and install it for you.
Who built this?
EduMind Uganda is built locally in Uganda, specifically for Ugandan students and schools. We understand the curriculum and the challenges schools face.
