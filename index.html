Yes. After the one-time setup, Vula works completely offline forever. No WiFi, no mobile data — ever again.
What PC do I need?
Windows 10 or Windows 11, 64-bit, with at least 4GB RAM and 5GB free disk space. Most laptops bought after 2015 will work.
How do I get my license key after paying?
After payment on WhatsApp Mobile Money, we send your license key within minutes. You type it once into Vula and it activates immediately.
Can I share my license with a friend?
No. Each license key is locked to one specific PC. If copied to another PC it will not work. Each device needs its own license.
How does the voice work?
Click the microphone button and speak naturally. Vula listens, thinks, and speaks back. You can also type if you prefer.
